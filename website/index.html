Bio

The Lampshades formed as teenagers in Altoona, Pennsylvania in Spring 2003. Today, they rehearse and record in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The band tours and releases new music as often as they can.

Dane Adelman - Drums/Production/Vocals
Chris Kibler - Bass/Vocals
Jaren Love - Lead Vocals/Guitar
